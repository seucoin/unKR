Bases: Matcher

Gaussian Metric Learning for Few-Shot Uncertain Knowledge Graph Completion (GMUC),

which proposes a novel method to complete few-shot UKGs based on Gaussian metric learning.

args

Model configuration parameters.

num_symbols

The sum of the number of entities and relationships.

embed

Pretrained embedding.
